monito/r/age

2019
Collabration with New Media artist Angelina Almukhametova
Found objects (two LCD televisions, a VGA computer monitor, analog surveillance camera and oscilloscope) each with their own unique condition, playing videos & texts on "Monitor Rage" aka "Computer Rage", which is the phenomenon where people will angrilly attack and destroy their devices when they do not perform as expected.
